Books to read

Atomic Habits

Author :James Clear

This book is all about forming good habits and breaking bad ones. It's a great read for anyone looking to improve themselves and their daily routines.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Author:Daniel Kahneman

This book dives into the way we think and make decisions, exploring the difference between our fast, intuitive thinking and our slower, more deliberate thinking. It's a fascinating read for anyone interested in psychology and decision-making.

The Alchemist

Author:Paulo Coelho

This classic book is a must-read for anyone looking for inspiration and a reminder to follow their dreams. It's a beautifully written story that encourages readers to pursue their passions.
